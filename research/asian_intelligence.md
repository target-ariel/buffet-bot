# Asian Market Intelligence: Hidden Trends (2025-2026)
Source: Chinese Industrial & Futures Reports (CASS, Green Dahua, NDRC)

## 1. The Copper Supply Cliff
Chinese analysts are projecting a **300,000-ton global refined copper deficit by 2026**. 
- **Key Factor:** Under-reported infrastructure accidents in Indonesia and Chile.
- **Sentiment:** China is front-running this by securing long-term supply contracts.
- **Stock Impact:** **FCX** (Freeport-McMoRan) is the primary beneficiary due to Grasberg ownership.

## 2. Uranium: Strategic Stockpiling
Unlike Western utilities which buy "just in time," China is moving to "Strategic Reserve" status.
- **Insight:** Potential for Chinese state-owned enterprises (SOEs) to take equity positions in major miners.
- **Sentiment:** Bullish. This provides a hard floor for Uranium prices.
- **Stock Impact:** **CCJ** (Cameco) and **KAP** (Kazatomprom).

## 3. Humanoid Robotics & Specialty Metals
Significant uptick in demand for **Rare Earths (NdFeB)** and **Tungsten** driven by the 2026 humanoid robot production scale-up in China.
- **Sentiment:** Watch for export restrictions as a geopolitical weapon.

## 4. Lithium Rebound Call
Chinese futures markets are signaling a "悄然改善" (quiet improvement) in supply-demand for Carbonate Lithium. 
- **Timeline:** Bottom likely reached in late 2025; recovery projected for H1 2026.
